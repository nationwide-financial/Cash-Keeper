What Is A Personal Loan?

An unsecured personal loan means borrowing funds without using assets like a house or car as collateral for a set duration. While the majority of loans from Cash Keeper Financial are unsecured, some credit unions might attach a lien to other accounts you have with them. Please ensure you thoroughly check your promissory note for such specifics before finalizing your loan.

Will This Affect My Credit Score?

When you check your rate at Cash Keeper Financial, your credit score remains unaffected. This is because we conduct a “soft credit inquiry,” which doesn’t decrease your score, though you might notice it listed when you review your report. However, if you decide to move forward with a loan application after checking your rate, a “hard credit inquiry” will be initiated to validate your application details. This inquiry will appear on your report and might influence your credit score.

Is There A Fee For Early Loan Payment?

At Cash Keeper Financial, you're free to pay off your loan early without any penalties. You can settle a portion or the full amount anytime, and there's no associated fee. By doing so, you'll reduce the total interest, given that the loan's duration becomes shorter.

What Can I Use A Personal Loan For?

Numerous individuals seek low-interest personal loans to merge their high-interest credit card dues. Such loans can also support significant life expenditures or investments, including home renovations, weddings, unforeseen medical bills, relocation costs, or funeral expenses.

Is Cash Keeper Financial Secured Or Unsecured?

Cash Keeper Financial are unsecured loans. This means that you do not need to provide collateral for the loan.
